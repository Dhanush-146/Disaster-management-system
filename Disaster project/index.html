Types of Disasters

Understanding different types of disasters is the first step in preparedness

Natural Disasters

Events caused by natural processes of the Earth:

  • Floods
  • Earthquakes
  • Hurricanes
  • Wildfires
  • Tornadoes
  • Tsunamis

Technological Disasters

Events resulting from technological failures:

  • Industrial accidents
  • Hazardous material spills
  • Nuclear incidents
  • Power outages
  • Infrastructure failures

Biological Disasters

Events involving biological agents:

  • Pandemics
  • Epidemics
  • Foodborne illnesses
  • Bioterrorism
  • Animal disease outbreaks

Preparedness & Safety

Essential steps to prepare for and stay safe during disasters

Home Preparedness

Make your home disaster-ready:

  • Create an emergency plan
  • Build an emergency kit
  • Secure heavy furniture
  • Install smoke detectors
  • Know utility shut-off locations

Emergency Kit

Essential items for your emergency kit:

  • Water (1 gallon/person/day)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First aid supplies
  • Flashlights & batteries
  • Important documents
  • Medications

Family Communication

Stay connected during emergencies:

  • Establish meeting points
  • Designate out-of-area contact
  • Save emergency numbers
  • Practice evacuation plans
  • Consider special needs
